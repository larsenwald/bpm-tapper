# BPM Tapper

Mic-powered BPM detector and metronome web app.

## How it works

Enable your mic, then snap your fingers to the beat. The app will detect transients in the audio input live and calculate your tempo from a rolling average of the last 8 intervals. Stop snapping for 2 seconds and it will reset.

## Metronome mode

Click **metronome →** to switch modes. Your last tapped BPM is carried over automatically. Drag up or down on the number to adjust, or use the scroll wheel. The BPM number pulses on every beat.
